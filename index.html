Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to rank on Google Maps? +
Most clients see significant ranking improvements within 7–21 days of GBP optimization. Some businesses rank in the Top 3 within 9 days. The timeline depends on your niche, city, and competition level.
What is Google Business Profile optimization? +
GBP optimization means making your Google Maps listing keyword-rich, complete, and actively managed so your business appears at the top when local customers search for your service. We research the best keywords for your business type and city, optimize every section, and help you rank higher on Google Maps.
Will I really get more calls and customers? +
Yes. When your business ranks in the Top 3 on Google Maps, you capture 70%+ of local search clicks. Our clients typically see a 2×–4× increase in calls and profile views within the first month of optimization.
What is Local SEO and how is it different from regular SEO? +
Local SEO focuses on ranking your business in local search results and Google Maps for location-specific queries like 'dentist near me' or 'gym in Lucknow'. Regular SEO targets broader organic rankings. Local SEO drives foot traffic and calls immediately.
How much does NexaFlow's services cost? +
Our services start from ₹499 for a basic GBP setup. Comprehensive local SEO packages, website SEO, and ongoing management plans are available at various price points. Contact us for a customized quote. A free audit is available with no commitment.
Can you help if my GBP has been suspended? +
Yes. We handle GBP reinstatements and suspensions regularly. Contact us via WhatsApp with your business details and we'll assess the situation and guide you through the reinstatement process step by step.
Do I need to provide content for my Google Business Profile? +
No. We handle everything — from writing keyword-rich descriptions and selecting the right categories, to creating Google Posts and setting up Q&A sections. All we need from you is basic business information and a few photos.
What is Website SEO and why does my business need it? +
Website SEO covers on-page optimization, technical SEO, content optimization, and link building to rank your website higher on Google search. Combining Website SEO with GBP optimization creates a powerful local dominance strategy that builds trust and converts more customers.
Do you guarantee Top 3 rankings? +
We do not make blanket guarantees as Google's algorithm is outside our control. However, 90%+ of our clients reach the Top 3 within 30 days when they follow our recommendations. We offer a free audit to assess your ranking potential before you commit.
How does the Review QR System work? +
Our Review QR System generates a custom QR code that directs your customers straight to your Google review page. Place it at your counter, on receipts, or in WhatsApp messages. Customers scan it in seconds and leave a review — dramatically increasing your review count and star rating.
Which areas do you serve? +
We serve businesses across all of India — with a focus on Lucknow, Kanpur, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Jaipur, and other major cities. Since GBP optimization is done remotely, we can help any business anywhere in India.
What is an SEO audit and do I need one? +
An SEO audit is a detailed analysis of your Google Business Profile, website, and online presence to identify exactly why you're not ranking and what needs to be fixed. We offer a free initial audit — you need one if you're not showing up on Google Maps or if your calls have dropped.
How do I track my ranking progress? +
We provide monthly performance reports showing your Google Maps ranking position, profile views, call clicks, direction requests, and website clicks. You'll always know exactly where you stand and what's improving each month.
Do you work with restaurants, clinics, salons, and retail shops? +
Absolutely. We work with all types of local businesses — restaurants, cafes, clinics, salons, gyms, coaching centres, real estate agencies, retail shops, hotels, and more. Any business that wants to be found on Google Maps can benefit from our services.
How is NexaFlow different from other SEO agencies? +
We specialize exclusively in Local SEO and Google Maps ranking — it's not a side service for us. We combine proven GBP optimization techniques with our proprietary Review QR System and data-driven SEO audits to deliver faster, more measurable results at transparent pricing.
Why does my business need a professional website? +
A professional website helps your business build trust, appear more credible, and attract more customers online. It also improves your visibility on Google, helps customers find your services easily, and works as a 24/7 digital storefront that can generate calls, leads, and enquiries anytime 🚀
