Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is Google Business Profile optimization and why does it matter? +
GBP optimization means making your Google Maps listing keyword-rich, complete, and actively managed — so when someone in your city searches for your service, your business shows up at the top instead of your competitor's. Most small businesses have incomplete profiles with no keywords, which is why they're invisible on Google Maps. We fix that.
What is WhatsApp automation for small businesses? +
WhatsApp automation uses AI to send instant replies, property/service details, and follow-up messages automatically when a customer contacts you — so you never miss a lead, even at 2am.
Does NexaFlow also build websites? +
Yes! We build clean, fast, mobile-ready websites for small businesses — gyms, coaching centres, real estate agents, salons, clinics, and more. Every website includes lead capture forms, WhatsApp integration, and is SEO-optimized from day one. Custom pricing based on your requirements — just chat with us on WhatsApp for a quote.

Plans start at just ₹499 for the GBP Boost Lite (one-time), ₹699 for the Starter WhatsApp setup, ₹1999/month for Growth, and ₹5499/month for Premium. Website development is custom-priced. A completely free online presence audit is also available — no credit card, no commitment.
Do I need technical skills or coding knowledge? +
Zero. NexaFlow sets everything up for you end-to-end. All you need is a smartphone and a WhatsApp Business account. We handle all the tech — you just start getting leads.
Which businesses can use NexaFlow AI? +
NexaFlow works for gyms, coaching centres, real estate agents, restaurants, salons, clinics, and any small business that wants to rank higher on Google Maps, get a professional website, or automate their WhatsApp lead flow.
