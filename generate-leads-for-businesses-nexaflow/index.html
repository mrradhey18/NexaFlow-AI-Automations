Questions Answered

How is AI lead generation different from normal lead gen? +
Traditional lead generation stops at getting you an enquiry. Our AI system captures the lead, sends them relevant information instantly, captures their details, and follows up automatically for 7–14 days — meaning every lead gets nurtured without any manual work from you.
Do I need a WhatsApp Business account? +
Yes. A WhatsApp Business account (free) is required for the automation. If you don't have one, we walk you through the setup in under 15 minutes during onboarding.
Can you generate leads for businesses outside Lucknow? +
Absolutely. NexaFlow is based in Lucknow but serves businesses across India — Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune and tier-2 cities. Everything is set up and managed remotely.
How long before I start seeing more leads? +
The WhatsApp automation and lead capture are live within 24–48 hours. Google Maps ranking improvements show within 7–14 days. Most clients see a significant uptick in qualified leads within the first 2–4 weeks.
